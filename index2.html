ABOUT TAKSHASHILA ACADEMY

Takshashila Academy was founded in 2015. The school is affiliated to West Bengal Board of Secondary Eductaion (W.B.B.S.E). Aim of School is to help the students to acquire sound knowledge of their subjects, to guide them for refinement of manners, uprightness and self restraint. To train the students to be gentle and courteous to their guardians, friends and neighbours, to respect and nourish culture of society, to develop the sense of responsibility. soliderity and above all to prepare them intellectually, morally and socially for their improvement as a total human beings in the world of the day. We solicite whole hearted cooperation of the Guardians.
